Popular Destinations

Mumbai

Visit the city that never sleeps—Mumbai. Explore iconic landmarks like the Gateway of India and Marine Drive, visit renowned museums like Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Vastu Sangrahalaya from Colaba to bustling Bandra.

Agra

Visit the city of timeless beauty—Agra. Explore iconic landmarks like the Taj Mahal and Agra Fort, visit renowned sites such as Fatehpur Sikri, and experience the rich history of its vibrant streets, from bustling markets to charming neighborhoods.

Assam

Visit the city of eternal charm—Agra. Discover iconic landmarks like the Taj Mahal and Agra Fort, explore renowned sites such as Fatehpur Sikri, and immerse yourself in the cultural richness of its vibrant bazaars and historic neighborhoods.
